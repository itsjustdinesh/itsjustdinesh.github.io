How it works — what we do & why

  1. What we do: Accept item + qty + location and show the lowest → medium → highest prices from supported quick-commerce apps (no backend).
  2. Why: Saves time — user no longer opens multiple delivery apps to check prices.
  3. Result: Price list sorted ascending. Clicking marketplace attempts app then falls back to website.
