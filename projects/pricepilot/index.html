How it works — what we do & why

  1. What we do: Accept item + qty + location and show the lowest → medium → highest prices from supported quick-commerce apps (no backend).
  2. Why: Saves time — user no longer opens multiple delivery apps to check prices.
  3. Result: Price list sorted ascending. Clicking a marketplace opens the marketplace search with the item query filled.
Tip: edit the PRICES dataset in the "Show data" box to reflect up-to-date prices you want to present on your portfolio.
